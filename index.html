Welcome to my website! I'm a final-year Master's student at the University of Calgary in Department of Computr Science. Under the guidance of Dr. Marina Gavrilova, I am engaged in researching human aesthetics for biometric identification. If you're interested in learning more about my research projects, feel free to visit my research page .

I have truly passionate about the captivating fields of computer vision, signal processing, natural language processing, deep learning, and machine learning. Throughout my journey, I have dedicated a significant portion of my work to leveraging the potential of deep learning techniques in various computer vision tasks. However, I have also eagerly explored other exciting areas such as security and privacy, natural language processing, and signal processing. I use transfer learning models, meta-heuristic feature optimization techniques, deep fusion models in my research.
